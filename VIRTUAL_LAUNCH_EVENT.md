# Virtual Launch Event Plan for Prism AI

## Overview

This document outlines the plan for hosting a virtual launch event for Prism AI, featuring a live product demo and interactive Q&A session. The event aims to showcase the product's capabilities, engage with early adopters, and build community around the launch.

## Event Objectives

1. **Product Showcase**: Demonstrate Prism AI's key features and capabilities
2. **Community Building**: Connect with early users and build a loyal community
3. **Feedback Collection**: Gather immediate feedback and feature requests
4. **Awareness Generation**: Increase visibility and attract new users
5. **Credibility Building**: Establish Prism AI as a serious player in the AI document analysis space

## Event Details

- **Event Name**: Prism AI Launch Event: Your Personal Research Assistant
- **Date**: [To be determined - recommend Tuesday-Thursday]

- Early adopters and beta testers

## Event Agenda (60 Minutes)

### Opening (5 minutes)
1. Welcome and introductions
2. Brief overview of the event agenda
3. Housekeeping announcements (Q&A process, recording, etc.)

### Company Introduction (10 minutes)
1. Founders/team introduction
2. Company mission and vision
3. Problem we're solving
4. Market opportunity

### Product Demo (20 minutes)
1. Document upload process
2. Question answering capabilities
3. Source citation feature
4. M-Pesa payment integration
5. Subscription plans and pricing

### Customer Testimonial (5 minutes)
1. Early user experience

### Closing and Next Steps (5 minutes)
1. Key takeaways
2. How to get started

4. Thank you and community invitation

## Technical Setup

### Primary Platform: Zoom Webinar
**Features Needed:**
- Up to 1,000 attendees
- Panelist video and screen sharing
- Attendee Q&A functionality
- Recording capabilities
- Breakout rooms (optional for smaller discussions)

**Setup Requirements:**
- Dedicated host/moderator account
- Panelist accounts for presenters
- Technical rehearsal before event
- Backup host in case of technical issues

### Backup Platform: YouTube Live
**Features:**
- Unlimited viewership
- Built-in chat functionality
- Automatic recording and archiving
- Integration with other Google services

### Audio/Visual Equipment
1. **Cameras**: HD webcam for each presenter
2. **Microphones**: Professional headset or lapel microphones
3. **Lighting**: Ring light or softbox lighting
4. **Screen Capture**: Reliable screen sharing software
5. **Internet**: Wired connection with backup mobile hotspot

### Rehearsal Schedule
- **1 Week Before**: Full technical rehearsal
- **3 Days Before**: Content rehearsal
- **1 Day Before**: Final equipment check
- **1 Hour Before**: Quick sound/lighting check

## Content Preparation

### Presentation Slides
1. **Title Slide**: Event name, date, company logo
2. **Agenda Slide**: Overview of what will be covered
3. **Company Introduction**: Mission, team, problem statement
4. **Market Opportunity**: Size, trends, competition
5. **Product Overview**: Key features and benefits
6. **Demo Walkthrough**: Step-by-step product tour
7. **Customer Testimonial**: Real user experience
8. **Roadmap**: Future plans and vision
9. **Pricing**: Subscription tiers and value proposition
10. **Call to Action**: How to get started
11. **Q&A**: Instructions for asking questions
12. **Closing**: Thank you, resources, contact info

### Demo Script
1. **Opening Hook**: "Imagine saving hours every week by simply asking questions about your documents"
2. **Problem Statement**: Show traditional document search process
3. **Solution Introduction**: Present Prism AI as the answer
4. **Step-by-Step Demo**:
   - Upload a sample document
   - Ask a question about the document
   - Show the answer with source citations
   - Demonstrate M-Pesa payment flow
5. **Key Features Highlight**:
   - Speed and accuracy
   - Security and privacy
   - Mobile payments
   - User-friendly interface
6. **Call to Action**: "Try Prism AI free today"

## Risk Management

### Technical Risks
- **Backup Plans**: YouTube Live as alternative platform
- **Equipment Failures**: Duplicate microphones and cameras
- **Internet Issues**: Mobile hotspot backup
- **Platform Outages**: Pre-recorded segments as fallback

### Content Risks
- **Presenter Absences**: Backup speakers identified
- **Demo Failures**: Pre-recorded demo as backup
- **Q&A Difficulties**: Prepared questions and responses
- **Time Management**: Strict timing with moderator

### Attendance Risks
- **Low Registration**: Aggressive promotion and reminders
- **Poor Attendance**: Incentives for attending (early access, discounts)
- **Engagement Issues**: Interactive elements and polls
- **Negative Feedback**: Professional handling and follow-up
