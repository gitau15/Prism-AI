# Social Media Launch Strategy for Prism AI

## Overview

This document outlines the social media launch strategy for Prism AI across Twitter/X, LinkedIn, and Facebook. The goal is to create buzz, drive traffic to the product, and attract early adopters through targeted, platform-specific content.

## Launch Timeline

### Pre-Launch (1 Week Before)
- Tease upcoming announcement
- Share behind-the-scenes content
- Build anticipation with feature spotlights

### Launch Day
- Coordinated announcement across all platforms
- Live updates and engagement
- User testimonials as they come in

### Post-Launch (First Week)
- Share user feedback and success stories
- Highlight interesting use cases
- Continue engagement with community

## Cross-Platform Coordination

### Timing Strategy
- Launch on Monday-Thursday for maximum visibility
- Post at optimal times for each platform:
  - Twitter: 12pm-3pm local time
  - LinkedIn: 10am-11am and 5pm-6pm weekdays
  - Facebook: 1pm-3pm weekdays

### Consistent Messaging
- Core value proposition remains the same
- Platform-specific adaptations in tone and format

- Unified call-to-action
