# Community Engagement Plan for Prism AI

## Objectives

1. Build awareness and excitement for Prism AI launch
2. Acquire early adopters and beta testers
3. Establish ongoing feedback loops with users
4. Create a loyal community around the product

## Target Communities

### Technical Communities

#### Product Hunt
- Primary launch platform
- Engage with tech enthusiasts and early adopters
- Leverage upvote and comment features
- Share product updates and milestones

#### Reddit
- r/SaaS - Software as a Service discussions
- r/MachineLearning - Technical AI/ML community
- r/Kenya - Local market targeting
- r/artificialintelligence - General AI discussions
- r/LocalLlama - AI model discussions

#### Hacker News
- Submit launch announcement
- Engage with technical feedback
- Monitor discussions and respond professionally

### Social Media Platforms

## Engagement Tactics

#### Teaser Campaign
- Share sneak peeks of the interface
- Post countdown to launch
- Share development progress updates
- Build anticipation with feature reveals

#### Influencer Outreach
- Contact tech bloggers and reviewers
- Reach out to AI/ML influencers

#### Beta Tester Recruitment
- Offer exclusive early access
- Create sense of exclusivity

- Provide incentives for participation

#### Coordinated Announcement
- Schedule announcements across all channels
- Prepare responses for common questions
- Monitor and engage with all comments

#### Live Events

## Community Management

### Moderation Guidelines

#### Code of Conduct
- Respectful communication standards
- No spam or promotional content
- Constructive criticism encouraged
- Harassment and hate speech prohibited

#### Response Protocols
- Acknowledge all community posts within 4 hours
- Resolve issues within 24 hours when possible
- Escalate complex issues to appropriate team members
- Document common questions for FAQ updates

#### Content Calendar
- Plan weekly content themes
- Schedule regular community events
- Align content with product releases
- Prepare seasonal and holiday content

### Growth Strategies

#### Referral Programs
- Incentivize user referrals
- Track referral sources
- Reward successful referrers
- Create viral loops

#### Partnership Opportunities
- Collaborate with universities
- Partner with research institutions
- Work with professional organizations
- Engage with government agencies

#### Content Amplification
- Cross-promote on all channels
- Encourage user sharing
- Leverage influencer networks
- Utilize paid promotion strategically
