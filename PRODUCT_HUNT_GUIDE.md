## Engagement Strategy

### Pre-Launch (1 week before)
- Reach out to potential upvoters in relevant communities
- Share teaser posts on social media
- Coordinate with tech influencers who might be interested
- Prepare responses to anticipated questions

### Launch Day
- Post goes live at 00:01 AM PST (best time for visibility)
- Founder/team should be active in comments for first 2-3 hours
- Share on all social channels with PH link
- Engage with every comment and upvote
- Share user testimonials as they come in

### Post-Launch (First week)
- Continue engaging with comments daily
- Share updates based on user feedback
- Post follow-up content showing usage statistics
- Thank upvoters publicly
- Address any issues or bugs transparently

## Hunter and Maker Accounts

### Hunter Account
- Should be someone credible in the tech/AI space
- Active on Product Hunt with previous launches
- Good network of upvoters and followers
- Willing to engage with community

### Maker Accounts
- Founders/developers of Prism AI
- Should be active on PH with history of engagement
- Ready to answer technical questions
- Available for real-time responses during launch

## Launch Timing

### Best Days to Launch
- Tuesday, Wednesday, or Thursday
- Avoid Mondays (low engagement) and Fridays (weekend approaching)

### Best Time to Launch
- 00:01 AM PST (Pacific Standard Time)
- This gives maximum visibility across global time zones

## Anticipated Questions & Responses

### Technical Questions
**Q: What AI models do you use?**
A: We use state-of-the-art transformer models from Hugging Face Sentence Transformers for embedding generation and support multiple LLMs including Gemini, GLM, and Mistral for answer generation.

**Q: How is privacy handled?**
A: Your documents are processed securely on our servers and are never shared with third parties. We implement industry-standard encryption and access controls.

**Q: What file formats do you support?**
A: We currently support PDF, DOCX, and PPTX files with plans to add more formats based on user demand.

### Business Questions
**Q: Why is this free to try?**
A: We believe in letting users experience the value before paying. Our freemium model allows everyone to try the core features while offering advanced capabilities in our Pro plan.

**Q: How does the M-Pesa integration work?**
A: Users can upgrade to Pro with a simple M-Pesa payment flow - just enter your phone number and complete the STK Push on your phone.

**Q: What's your monetization strategy?**
A: We offer two subscription tiers: a free Explorer plan and a Pro plan priced at KES 499/month. Future enterprise plans are planned for larger teams.

### Feature Requests
**Q: Will you support [specific feature]?**
A: We're constantly improving based on user feedback. Please submit your feature requests through our feedback system and we'll consider them for future releases.
