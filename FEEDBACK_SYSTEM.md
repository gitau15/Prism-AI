# Feedback Collection System for Prism AI

## Feedback Channels

### 1. Email
- Dedicated email address: feedback@prism-ai.com
- Monitored daily by the product team
- Responses within 24 hours for all feedback

### 2. In-App Feedback Button
- Prominently placed in the user interface
- Simple form with:
  - Rating scale (1-5 stars)
  - Open text feedback
  - Feature request option
  - Bug reporting option

### 3. Typeform Survey
- Sent to users after 3 days of usage
- Questions:
  - How has Prism AI helped you save time?
  - What features would you like to see added?
  - How likely are you to recommend Prism AI to others?
  - Any specific issues or frustrations?

### 4. Social Media Monitoring
- Track mentions on Twitter/X
- Monitor comments on Product Hunt
- Watch relevant subreddit discussions
- Respond to all mentions within 4 hours during business hours

## Feedback Categorization

### Bug Reports
- Technical issues preventing normal usage
- Priority: Critical (fix within 24 hours) or High (fix within 72 hours)

### Feature Requests
- Suggestions for new functionality
- Categorized by frequency and user impact

### User Experience Issues
- Confusing workflows or unclear interfaces
- Prioritized by impact on user success

### General Comments
- Positive feedback and testimonials
- General observations about the product

## Response Protocol

### Initial Response
- Acknowledge receipt of all feedback within 2 hours
- For bugs: Provide estimated timeline for fix
- For feature requests: Explain whether it's under consideration

### Resolution Process
- Bugs: Fixed and deployed according to priority
- Feature requests: Evaluated weekly by product team
- UX issues: Reviewed and addressed in next sprint

### Follow-Up
- Notify users when their reported bugs are fixed
- Update users on status of feature requests
- Thank users for positive feedback

## Feedback Analysis

### Weekly Reports
- Compile all feedback received during the week
- Identify trends and common themes
- Highlight urgent issues requiring immediate attention
- Track sentiment over time

### Monthly Metrics
- Total feedback volume
- Bug report frequency
- Feature request popularity
- User satisfaction trends
- Net Promoter Score (NPS) calculation

## Tools and Infrastructure

### Email Management
- Google Workspace for professional email handling
- Templates for common response types
- Labels and filters for categorization

### Survey Platform
- Typeform for user surveys
- Automated sending based on user behavior
- Analytics dashboard for response tracking

### Social Media Monitoring
- Manual monitoring of key platforms
- Google Alerts for brand mentions
- Consider Hootsuite or similar tool as volume increases

### Internal Tracking
- GitHub Issues for bug tracking
- Notion or similar tool for feature request management
- Shared spreadsheet for feedback categorization and analysis

## Community Building

### Early Adopter Program
- Special recognition for first 100 users
- Exclusive access to new features
- Direct line to product team

### Beta Tester Group
- Curated group of engaged users
- Early access to major updates
- Regular video calls to discuss product direction

### Ambassador Program
- Incentives for users who refer others
- Special badges or recognition in the app
- Exclusive swag for top ambassadors

## Continuous Improvement

### Regular Review Meetings
- Weekly 1-hour meeting to review feedback
- Monthly strategic session to prioritize improvements
- Quarterly retrospective on feedback system effectiveness

### Process Optimization
- Regular evaluation of response times
- Assessment of resolution effectiveness
- Updates to categorization and prioritization criteria

### User Communication
- Monthly newsletter highlighting improvements based on feedback
- Transparency reports on feature implementation
- Recognition of users whose feedback led to significant improvements
